3. Summary

Needleman–Wunsch is used for global alignment (entire sequence), while Smith–Waterman focuses on local alignment (best matching region).

  • Needleman–Wunsch is slower but aligns full sequences.
  • Smith–Waterman is faster and finds best matching subsequences.
